Sound files needed for the Milionerzy game.

Required files:
- correct.mp3 - Sound for correct answer
- incorrect.mp3 - Sound for wrong answer
- timer-warning.mp3 - Warning sound when time is running low
- achievement.mp3 - Achievement unlock sound
- streak.mp3 - Streak bonus sound
- click.mp3 - Button click sound
- background.mp3 - Background music (optional)

Suggested sources for free sounds:
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
- https://freesound.org/
- https://www.zapsplat.com/

Note: The game will work without these files - sounds will simply not play.
